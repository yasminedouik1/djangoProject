Our Story

Mamma Mia was founded in 2024 by Yasmine Douik, a passionate food enthusiast with a deep love for Italian cuisine. Inspired by her travels to Italy and her desire to bring authentic flavors to her hometown in Tunis, Yasmine created Mamma Mia to share her culinary passion. Using recipes she learned from Italian chefs and adding her own creative twist, she built a cozy space where every dish tells a story of tradition and love. Today, Mamma Mia is a cherished spot for locals and visitors alike, offering a true taste of Italy with a warm, personal touch.
